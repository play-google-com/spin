  • From when will GST start getting charged?

    All players deposit shall be subject to GST at the rate of 28% to the government on every deposit (Add Cash) from 1st October 2023 onwards. This is applicable for all players on online money gaming apps

  • Who is charging this GST?

    The government is charging GST at the rate of 28% on every deposit (Add Cash). Playship plays no role in taxes applied by the government.

  • How do I deposit the GST amount to the goverment?

    During deposit (Add Cash), the platform will automatically calculate the GST amount. This amount will be submitted paid to the government. As a proof, a GST invoice will be generated and provided to all players. This will be visible and can be downloaded from the Add Cash success screen and the Wallet section.

However, if you have available buy in balance in the form of Discount Credits then the entry will not be free. Discount Credits equal to the buy in value of that game will be applied from your wallet.
