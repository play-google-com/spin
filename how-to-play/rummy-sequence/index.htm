What is Sequence?

At least three cards of the same suit in the same group are considered a sequence. There are a few other names for this, run and first sequence being the most common ones.

Examples of sequence

How Many Types of Sequences are there in Rummy?

There are 2 Types of Sequence in the Rummy- Pure and Impure sequence.

What is a pure sequence in rummy?

A sequence of at least three cards of the same suit is called a pure sequence. You cannot use a joker card to complete a pure sequence. The other common name for a pure sequence is – first life.

What is Impure Sequence in Rummy?

A sequence that has been created with the help of a joker card, is called an impure sequence.

Important rules in rummy regarding the sequence

  • 1

    The sequence has to be a minimum of 3 Cards.

  • 2

    A sequence can even have 4 or 5 cards in it.

  • 3

    To make a valid declaration, you need at least 2 sequences.

  • 4

    Also, one of those sequences should be a pure sequence.

  • 5

    Printed and wild card jokers can be used to make impure sequences.
