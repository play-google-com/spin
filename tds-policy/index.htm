About this Policy

In line with the provisions contained in Finance Act, 2023, the following TDS policy is applicable for all players on Playship Rummy from 1 Apr, 2023.

  • TDS is applicable at the time of withdrawal and also at the year end
  • 30% TDS is applicable on net winnings at the time of withdrawal and also at the year end

How winnings is calculated?

Net winnings : Total Withdrawals - Total Deposits* (in a financial year i.e 1 Apr to 31 Mar)

* Total deposits refers deposits in the financial year + opening balance of financial year

Any net winnings which have suffered TDS at the time of any earlier withdrawal would also be reduced while computing Net winnings at the time of withdrawal

Please note:

The TDS Policy is based on the understanding of the provisions as introduced/ amended by Finance Act, 2023 and the Company reserves its right to modify/ change/ amend the TDS Policy basis law applicable at the relevant time.

We are often asked what happens to the balance lying in an account at end of the financial year.

Scenario 6 :

At the end of the financial year, if you have some amount lying in your wallet (excluding the amount on the table), then TDS will be applied to that amount by considering it as a withdrawal for the financial year. The remaining amount will be carried forward to the next year as an opening balance. A withdrawal from the opening balance will not be subjected to TDS in the next year.

For example, let us assume in a financial year:
Total deposits in the financial year = ₹40,000
Total withdrawals in the financial year = ₹40,000
TDS paid till date (on winning amount of ₹5,000 as initially you had positive net winnings of ₹5,000) = ₹1,500
Account balance at the end of 31 Mar, 2024 = ₹30,000

In this situation, following calculation will be done in your account:

Total Withdrawal amount (A)Withdrawals of ₹40,000 + ₹30,000 a/c balance

₹70,000

Total deposits (B)

₹40,000

Net winnings ( A- B)

₹30,000

Net winnings previously taxed

₹5,000

Taxable net winnings (₹30,000- ₹5000)

₹25,000

TDS on net winnings 30% on ₹25000

₹7,500

  • Thus, a TDS of ₹7,500 is applied to your account balance at the end of 31 Mar, 2024 and the remaining balance of ₹22,500 (₹30,000- ₹7,500) will be carried forward to the next financial year as a opening balance. TDS will not be applied in the next financial year on withdrawals of ₹22,500.

Special Note :

  • In case, you have an amount on the table at the end of the financial year, then it will not be considered in next year's opening balance.
